blank_waves [Twitter bot]

A bot built with Node.js. Every 30 minutes, the bot tweets a randomly selected image from an archived collection of various art and aesthetic images. The purpose of this project is to provide followers with short reflective visual experiences amidst the constant stream of words on Twitter, a beautiful and strange absence of words in a space full of only words.

Idioteque [Jekyll Theme]

A minimal and responsive Jekyll blog theme built with Bourbon, Sass, Gulp.js and statically generated comments. Idioteque is very versitile in its minimalism, designed to easily customize colors and design patterns through the use of Sass mixins and variables, as instructed in the README file of the repository.
